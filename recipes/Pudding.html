Chocolate pudding


Description

A vegan chocolate pudding. Made from avocado

Ingredients

  • 2 large avocados - peeled, pitted, and cubed
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup coconut milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Blend avocados, cocoa powder, brown sugar, coconut milk, vanilla, and cinnamon in a blender until smooth. Transfer pudding to a container with a lid, then refrigerate until chilled, about 30 minutes.
